About Us

Empowering Financial Freedom

SHIB BANK is a next-generation digital bank built to deliver secure, instant, and smart money transfers for individuals and businesses across the globe.

10+ Years of Excellence

Founded with a vision to democratize financial services, SHIB BANK combines cutting-edge technology with a customer-first approach. We believe that everyone deserves access to fast, secure, and affordable banking — whether you're sending money to a loved one, paying suppliers across borders, or managing payroll for your team.

Our platform is built on trust, transparency, and innovation. With bank-grade security, real-time processing, and a suite of financial tools, we're redefining what it means to bank in the digital age.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about SHIB BANK.

How do I open an account?
Opening an account is quick and easy. Simply click the "Open Account" button, fill in your details, verify your identity, and you'll be ready to start banking in minutes.
Is SHIB BANK safe and secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption, two-factor authentication, and fraud monitoring systems to ensure every transaction is fully protected.
What countries do you support?
We currently support over 10 countries across Africa and Europe, with more regions being added regularly. Check our coverage map for the full list.
How long do transfers take?
Most transfers are processed instantly. In some cases, international transfers may take up to 24 hours depending on the destination and compliance checks.
Do you offer loans and credit?
Yes, we offer business loans, consumer financing, and credit facilities up to KES 5M for eligible customers. Contact our team to learn more.
How do I contact support?
Our support team is available 24/7 via live chat, email (support@shibbank.com), or phone. You can also reach out through our contact form below.
